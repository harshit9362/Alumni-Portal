The Highlights of Amritotsavam

The highlight of Amrita’s colorful celebrations was a procession in which all students, staff and residents of the Coimbatore campus participated. The procession included beautiful floats depicting various incidents in Maha Vishnu’s and Sri Krishna’s life and exploits like Mahishasura Vadham, Sundarakandam, Dashavatharam, Kaliya Narthanam, Vishwaroopa Darshanam and Mantralayam. Motivating healthy competition and bringing out the creative and artistic potential and talents of the students, various competitions in sports, literary and cultural events were also conducted in addition to the float competition.
