ANOKHA


The School of Engineering, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore campus, organized the 10th National-level Techfest, Anokha 2020, from February 13-15, 2020.

ABOUT ANOKHA


“Anokha Techfest, the annual event of Amrita School of Engineering, Coimbatore provides an unique opportunity for students to participate in fun-filled and challenging events and workshops. The event throws open opportunities for students from all disciplines to challenge themselves and foster the “Can Do” attitude. One of the key highlights of the Anokha 2020 edition was the Green Anokha concept. It was so wonderful to see the students adopt and implement our Chancellor AMMA’s idea of “Living in Harmony with Nature”. It was plastic-free and eco-friendly Anokha – an initiative that will become a tradition!!”, Dr. Sasangan Ramanathan, Dean, Faculty of Engineering, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. ...

1) 10th edition of the techfest of Amrita School of Engineering, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore held from February 13-15, 2020.

2) Special event as it is happening on the silver jubilee year of the Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore campus.

3) Tag lines of techfest are “Dare to be Different” and “Bigger, Better & Greener”.

4) One of India’s top techfests and first to have United Nations Academic Impact (UNAI) accreditation.

5) Participation by thousands of students all over India.

6) Major sponsors of the 2020 edition included Cognizant, Analog Devices, H&R Block, Infoview, State Bank of India (SBI) and SkillsDA.

7) 50 highly competitive events in all branches of engineering, gaming and robotics. Popular contests and competitions included Robowarz, Amrita’s version of Battlebots, Death race, Autonomous robots & Drone challenge, Arduino U know, Hydropowered Rocket, Paper wings, Robosoccer, King of Concrete and master of circuits.
